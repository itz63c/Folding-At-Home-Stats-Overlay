A modern, lightweight Windows overlay to track your Folding@Home contributions in real time without getting in your way.

Key Features:

Live Stats: Displays your total points, current rank, and your top percentage among users. The app syncs with the public API every 5 minutes.
Session Tracking: Automatically calculates and displays exactly how many points you have earned since launching the application.
Modern Interface: A sleek, dark-themed, borderless window. Just click anywhere on the background to drag and position it perfectly on your screen.
Always on Top: Use the custom toggle switch to pin the overlay above your other windows, or uncheck it to let it fall to the background.
Smart Memory: Type your Folding@Home username once, and the app saves it locally for your next session.
Standalone Executable: Compiled directly to an .exe so you do not have to mess with Windows PowerShell execution policies.


Double-click to run. No installation required.
Enter your Folding@Home username in the top left, click "Set", and let it track your progress!
